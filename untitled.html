History of Kerala

Kerala's rich history stretches back over 3000 years. Known for the spice trade, it attracted explorers and traders from Greece, Rome, Arabia, and China. Influenced by the Chera dynasty and colonial powers, Kerala emerged as a culturally unique region.

Traditional Arts

Kerala's traditional arts include vibrant dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam. Theyyam, Pulikali, and Chakyar Koothu are celebrated during festivals, showcasing Kerala’s spiritual and artistic roots.

Top Destinations

Discover breathtaking places like Munnar's tea gardens, Alleppey's houseboats, Wayanad’s forests, and the serene beaches of Kovalam.
