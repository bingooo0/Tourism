History of Kerala

Ancient Roots

Kerala's civilization dates back over 3000 years, flourishing along the Arabian Sea with a strong spice trade network. It was known to Greeks, Romans, Arabs, and Chinese who visited its shores for black pepper and exotic goods.

Cultural Influences

The Chera dynasty ruled much of Kerala in early centuries, laying foundations for art, architecture, and language. Later, colonial powers like the Portuguese, Dutch, and British left their mark, blending East and West.
